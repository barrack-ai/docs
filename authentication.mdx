## Login Process

### Email & Password Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Login Page">
    Navigate to [barrack.ai/login](https://barrack.ai/login)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    - Enter your registered email address
    - Enter your secure password
    - Optional: Check "Remember me" for convenience
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Identity">
    Complete any additional security verification if prompted
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard">
    Successfully log in to your [dashboard](https://barrack.ai/dashboard)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### OTP (One-Time Password) Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request OTP">
    - Navigate to [login page](https://barrack.ai/login)
    - Click "Login with OTP"
    - Enter your registered email address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email">
    - Check your email inbox for the OTP code
    - The code is valid for 10 minutes
    - Codes are single-use only
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter OTP">
    - Return to the login page
    - Enter the 6-digit OTP code
    - Click "Verify and Login"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Account">
    Successfully access your account with enhanced security
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Google OAuth Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate OAuth">
    - Navigate to [login page](https://barrack.ai/login)
    - Click "Continue with Google"
    - You'll be redirected to Google's authentication
  </Step>
  <Step title="Google Authentication">
    - Log in to your Google account if not already logged in
    - Review and accept the permissions requested
    - Authorize Barrack to access your basic profile information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Platform">
    - You'll be automatically redirected back to Barrack
    - Your account will be created or accessed automatically
    - Access your dashboard immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Creation

<Steps>

    Navigate to [barrack.ai/signup](https://barrack.ai/signup)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Registration Method">

    - **Google OAuth:** Register using your Google account

  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Required Information">
    - Valid email address (for communications)

  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    - Access your [account page](https://barrack.ai/account)
    - Complete billing information (required for purchases)
    - Add contact information and preferences
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Password Recovery Process

If you forget your password or need to reset it:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Recovery">
    - Go to the [login page](https://barrack.ai/login)
    - Click "Forgot Password?" link
    - Enter your registered email address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email">
    - Check your email for the password reset link
    - The link is valid for 24 hours
    - Click the secure reset link provided
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Password">
    - Enter a new secure password
    - Confirm the new password
    - Ensure it meets all security requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Reset">
    - Complete the password reset process
    - Log in with your new password
    - Update any saved password managers
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Password reset links expire after 24 hours for security. If you don't reset within this time, you'll need to request a new reset link.
</Warning>

## Security Best Practices

### Password Requirements

When creating or updating your password, ensure it meets these security requirements:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Minimum Requirements">
    - **Length:** At least 8 characters
    - **Uppercase:** At least 1 uppercase letter (A-Z)
    - **Lowercase:** At least 1 lowercase letter (a-z)
    - **Numbers:** At least 1 numeric digit (0-9)
    - **Special Characters:** At least 1 special character (!@#$%^&*)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Recommended Practices">
    - **Length:** 12+ characters for stronger security
    - **Uniqueness:** Don't reuse passwords from other services
    - **Complexity:** Mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
    - **Unpredictability:** Avoid common words or patterns
    - **Regular Updates:** Change passwords periodically
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Avoid These">
    - Personal information (birthdays, names, addresses)
    - Common passwords (password123, qwerty, etc.)
    - Dictionary words without modifications
    - Sequential patterns (123456, abcdef)
    - Keyboard patterns (qwerty, asdf)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting Authentication Issues

### Common Login Problems

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Forgotten Password">
    **Problem:** Can't remember your password
    
    **Solution:**
    1. Use the "Forgot Password?" link on [login page](https://barrack.ai/login)
    2. Enter your registered email address
    3. Check email for reset instructions
    4. Follow the secure reset process
    5. Create a new, strong password
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Email Not Received">
    **Problem:** Not receiving verification or reset emails
    
    **Solution:**
    1. Check your spam/junk folder
    2. Verify the email address is correct
    3. Wait a few minutes for email delivery
    4. Check with your email provider about blocking
    5. Try requesting another email
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="OAuth Issues">
    **Problem:** Google OAuth not working
    
    **Solution:**
    1. Ensure you're logged into the correct Google account
    2. Check your browser's popup blocker settings
    3. Clear browser cache and cookies
    4. Try using a different browser
    5. Verify Google account permissions
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Account Locked">
    **Problem:** Account appears to be locked or suspended
    
    **Solution:**
    1. Wait a few minutes and try again
    2. Check email for security notifications
    3. Contact support if issue persists
    4. Provide account details for faster resolution
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
